## What is this?

This repository contains *Field Notes from the Substrate — Translations No. 8: ¯\\(ツ)/¯-Licensed E = mc²*.

This note gives a Structural Flow translation of the rest-frame mass-energy relation usually written as:

**E₀ = mc²**

E = mc² is one of the most famous equations in physics, but it is often compressed too quickly.

This note slows the equation down. It argues that the rest-frame relation concerns a bounded whole that does not carry one independent burden as inertia and another independent burden as rest-energy.

Instead, in its rest frame, the bounded whole carries one whole-owned invariant read in two registers.

## What is the claim?

The claim is narrow:

Structural Flow identifies the structural object classically expressed by the rest-frame equation E₀ = mc².

That object is:

**a Coherence-closed bounded rest-frame whole carrying one whole-owned invariant, of which inertial answerability and total bound rest-energy are two registers.**

Put more simply:

**one bounded whole, one invariant, two registers: inertia and rest-energy.**

## What is this not?

This is not a derivation of all relativity.

It does not derive the full energy-momentum relation, the measured value of c, the full stress-energy tensor, general relativity, particle masses, the Higgs mechanism, quantum field theoretic mass generation, or every concrete binding-energy mechanism.

It is not the claim that all uses of mass, energy, inertia, or rest-energy are interchangeable.

It is a rest-frame translation note.
